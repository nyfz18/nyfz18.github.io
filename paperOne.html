Fundamentals of AI applications in Patient Care

By Nina Zhang

With the rapid growth of artificial intelligence (AI), new technologies are transforming the medical field at an unprecedented pace. This article will explore how AI is being integrated into clinical decision-making, diagnostics, and healthcare delivery. It will introduce core concepts used in these medical settings, such as machine learning, predictive modeling, and natural language processing.


While AI offers significant benefits, from supporting physicians, streamlining workflows, and improving patient outcomes, it also raises important ethical concerns, including bias, accountability, and transparency. It is essential for responsible and equitable implementation of these new technologies to ensure AI contributes postively and fairly to patient care.
